Let's Get Started!

  1. To start a group, a designated user will register as an administrator.
  2. Upon registration, the administrator will be assigned a unique group ID# to hand out to members in the group.
  3. Each group member will register using the ID# to join.
  4. Vote conveniently, in person or remote!
